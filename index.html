About Me

Wildan Fauzy is an author from Indonesia. Write novels, short stories, essays. Pig pen and piglet book a collection of stories.

Wildan was born in Cirebon, West Java, Indonesia, on March 26, 1996. Raised in a small village called Budur, about an hour's drive from the city of Cirebon.

His journey as a writer began with his passion for storytelling and capturing the essence of everyday life. Through his unique perspective and creative narration, Wildan brings characters to life in his works.
