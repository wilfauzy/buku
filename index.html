WILDAN FAUZY

About the Author

Wildan Fauzy is an author from Indonesia who writes novels, short stories, and essays. His notable works include "Kandang Babi" (Pig Pen) and "Anak Babi" (Piglet).

Born in Cirebon, West Java, Indonesia, on March 26, 1996, Wildan was raised in a small village called Budur, about an hour's drive from the city of Cirebon.

His unique storytelling style and creative narrative have made him a distinctive voice in Indonesian literature.
