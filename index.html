The Art of Pixel Design

Posted on March 2, 2025 | Category: Pixel Art

Pixel art has been making a comeback in recent years, with many indie games and digital artists embracing the retro aesthetic. From its humble beginnings in early video games to the sophisticated pixel art we see today, this art form has evolved while maintaining its characteristic charm.

Creating good pixel art requires attention to detail and an understanding of the limitations and strengths of the medium. Unlike other digital art forms, pixel art is created pixel by pixel, with each dot carefully placed to contribute to the whole image.
